# Privacy Policy

PostFlow is a private tool used solely by its owner. The app only accesses TikTok data required for authentication and uploading original video content as drafts to the user’s own TikTok account.

No personal data is sold, shared, or used for advertising. Access tokens are stored securely and used only to provide the app’s intended functionality. The app does not collect analytics or track user behavior beyond what is required to operate.
